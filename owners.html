We are in the path of Leading Forward Horizons

Pradeep Deverakonda

Founder and CEO, MD

As the founder of Krupa Corporation, I am excited to welcome you to our journey in the construction industry. My path to this venture began with a passion for solving complex problems and enhancing efficiency within the construction ecosystem. After working on several business projects, I made the bold decision to drop out of college, dedicating myself to rigorous research and development focused on creating smart construction solutions.

At Krupa Corporation, our mission is to revolutionize the way building materials are procured and managed. We recognize the challenges that builders and SMEs face in navigating the supply chain, and we are committed to providing seamless, efficient, and cost-effective procurement solutions. Our approach leverages technology to streamline processes, ensuring that our clients can focus on what they do best—building and creating.

We are driven by the vision of building the next big thing in the construction industry. Our commitment to excellence and innovation fuels our ambition to set new benchmarks, transforming the landscape of construction procurement. With every solution we develop, we strive to not only meet the evolving needs of our clients but also to redefine what is possible in this space.

As we move forward, I invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Together, we can transform the construction landscape, empowering businesses to achieve their goals and create a lasting impact. Thank you for being a part of our vision, and I look forward to the future we will build together.

Thank you.
